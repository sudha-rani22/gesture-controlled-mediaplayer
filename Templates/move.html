Instructions to use:

1.Open the VLC Media Player.
2.Open the video of your choice.
3.[Very Important!!!]
The cursor must be placed on the screen of the vlc media player and
click on it while showing gestures.
4.Now show the following gestures to perform the functions.

Gestures to be shown:

Swipe right with one finger-Go forward
Swipe left with two fingers-Go backward
Swipe up with three fingers-Increase volume
Swipe down with four fingers-Decrease volume
Open hand-Play/Pause
